Stainless Steel Ball Valve Tags identify ball valves that use a spherical closure element. Stainless Steel Globe Valve Tags are for globe valves that regulate flow direction. Stainless Steel Butterfly Valve Tags denote butterfly valves, which offer quick shut-off. Other tags may include specialized designs for unique valve types or applications, enhancing identification and management in various industries.</p></p>
