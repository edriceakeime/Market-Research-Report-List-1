<p><p>The Printed Circuit Board (PCB) laminate market comprises several key types. FR-4 is the most common, made from fiberglass-reinforced epoxy due to its excellent electrical properties and thermal stability. High Tg epoxy offers higher glass transition temperatures for demanding applications. BT epoxy provides enhanced thermal performance and lower dielectric loss. Polyimide is flexible and suitable for high-performance environments. Copper clad laminates combine copper and substrate for wiring, while Teflon is used for high-frequency applications due to its low dielectric constant. Other types include specialty materials for unique needs.</p></p>
