<p><p>The Ultra-Violet Fluorescent Ink market is categorized into two main types: Invisible Ultraviolet Fluorescent Ink and Visible Ultraviolet Fluorescent Ink. Invisible Ultraviolet Fluorescent Ink is used primarily for security applications, as it remains undetectable under normal light but fluoresces under UV light, making it ideal for anti-counterfeiting measures. In contrast, Visible Ultraviolet Fluorescent Ink emits a vibrant glow under UV light, making it suitable for creative projects, signage, and promotional materials that aim to attract attention and enhance visibility.</p></p>
