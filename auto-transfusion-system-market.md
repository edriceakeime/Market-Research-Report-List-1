<p><strong>The Auto Transfusion System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Continuous Autotransfusion Systems</li><li>Discontinuous Autotransfusion Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Auto Transfusion System market is primarily divided into Continuous Autotransfusion Systems and Discontinuous Autotransfusion Systems. Continuous systems facilitate the ongoing collection and reinfusion of a patient’s blood during surgery, ensuring immediate availability and reducing the need for donor blood. In contrast, Discontinuous systems involve collecting blood at specific intervals, allowing for reinfusion later, which may suit certain surgical procedures. Both types enhance patient safety and optimize blood management, addressing the growing demand for autotransfusion in medical settings.</p></p>
