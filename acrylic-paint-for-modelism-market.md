<p><p>Acrylic paint for modelism is categorized into three main types: heavy body, soft body, and fluid paint. Heavy body acrylics have a thick, buttery consistency, ideal for texture and impasto techniques. Soft body acrylics are more fluid, providing a smooth application while maintaining color vibrancy, perfect for detail work. Fluid acrylics are the thinnest, allowing for washes and fine lines, making them suitable for intricate designs. Each type offers unique qualities that cater to different modeling techniques and artistic styles.</p></p>
