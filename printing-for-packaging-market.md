<p><ul><li>Flexography Printing</li><li>Rotogravure Printing</li><li>Digital Printing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The printing for packaging market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **Flexography Printing**: Utilizes flexible relief plates for high-speed production and is ideal for large print runs on various substrates, making it popular for packaging materials like labels and cartons.</p><p>2. **Rotogravure Printing**: Employs engraved cylinders for high-quality, continuous prints, suitable for packaging films and large volumes, especially in food packaging.</p><p>3. **Digital Printing**: Offers versatility with on-demand production and personalization, allowing for quick adjustments and smaller print runs, increasingly adopted for flexible packaging solutions.</p></p>
