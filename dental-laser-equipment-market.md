<p><p>The dental laser equipment market is primarily divided into two types: soft tissue dental lasers and all tissue dental lasers. Soft tissue lasers are specifically designed for procedures involving gums and soft tissues, providing precise cutting and coagulation with minimal bleeding and discomfort. In contrast, all tissue lasers can handle both soft and hard tissues, enabling a wider range of dental procedures, including cavity preparation and bone surgery. Both types enhance surgical outcomes and patient comfort through less invasive techniques.</p></p>
