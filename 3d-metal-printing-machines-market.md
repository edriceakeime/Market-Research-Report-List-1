<p><p>The 3D metal printing machines market features several key types, including Powder Bed Fusion, Electron Beam Melting (EBM), and Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS). Powder Bed Fusion utilizes a laser to selectively fuse powdered metal layers. EBM employs an electron beam in a vacuum environment, allowing for the melting of metal powder to create parts with superior mechanical properties. DMLS, similar to Powder Bed Fusion, utilizes a laser to sinter metal powders, offering high precision and complexity in designs for various industrial applications.</p></p>
