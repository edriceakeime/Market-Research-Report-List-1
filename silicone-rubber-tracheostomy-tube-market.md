<p><p>The Silicone Rubber Tracheostomy Tube market includes three main types: outer cannulas, inner cannulas, and obturators. Outer cannulas serve as the primary support structure for the tracheostomy tube, ensuring airflow and stability. Inner cannulas are removable components that provide easy cleaning and maintenance, allowing for better hygiene. Obturators are used for facilitating the insertion of the tracheostomy tube, ensuring a smoother process. Together, these components enhance patient comfort and care in respiratory management.</p></p>
