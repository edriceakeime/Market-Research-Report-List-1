<p><strong>The Powder Bed Fusion Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS)</li><li>Selective Heat Sintering (SHS)</li><li>Selective Laser Sintering (SLS)</li><li>Electron Beam Melting (EBM)</li><li>Selective Laser Melting (SLM)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Powder Bed Fusion market encompasses various advanced additive manufacturing techniques. Direct Metal Laser Sintering (DMLS) uses lasers to sinter metal powders, offering high precision for complex parts. Selective Heat Sintering (SHS) melts polymer powders for durable prototypes. Selective Laser Sintering (SLS) uses lasers to fuse polymer powders, creating strong components. Electron Beam Melting (EBM) utilizes an electron beam for metal parts, emphasizing rapid production. Selective Laser Melting (SLM) fully melts metal powders for dense and intricate geometries, enhancing mechanical properties.</p></p>
