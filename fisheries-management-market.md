<p><p>Fisheries management markets can be categorized into on-site and off-site treatment options. On-site treatment involves managing fish populations directly within their natural habitat, focusing on practices like habitat restoration, fishing quotas, and sustainable harvesting methods to ensure the health of the ecosystem. Off-site treatment, conversely, refers to managing fish populations in controlled environments, such as aquaculture facilities, where breeding, feeding, and health monitoring can be optimized. Both approaches aim to enhance sustainability and maintain fish stocks for future use.</p></p>
