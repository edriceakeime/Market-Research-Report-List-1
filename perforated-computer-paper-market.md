<p><ul><li>Single Layer</li><li>Multi-layer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The perforated computer paper market is divided into two main types: single-layer and multi-layer paper. Single-layer paper consists of a single sheet perforated for easy feeding through printers, ideal for simple printing tasks. In contrast, multi-layer paper features multiple sheets, typically used for forms requiring duplicates, such as invoices or shipping labels. This type allows for simultaneous printing on several layers, ensuring accurate replication of information. Both types cater to different needs in printing and documentation processes.</p></p>
