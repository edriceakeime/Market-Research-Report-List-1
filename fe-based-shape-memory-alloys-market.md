<p><p>Fe-based shape memory alloys (SMAs) are classified primarily into two market types: high temperature austenite phase and low temperature martensite phase. The high temperature austenite phase exhibits excellent mechanical properties and stability under elevated temperatures, making it suitable for aerospace and automotive applications. In contrast, the low temperature martensite phase is characterized by its ability to recover significant shape changes at lower temperatures, making it ideal for applications in biomedical devices and consumer products. Both phases offer distinct advantages depending on the intended application.</p></p>
