<p><p>The medical imaging system market encompasses various technologies essential for diagnosing and monitoring diseases. X-ray devices utilize ionizing radiation to visualize internal structures. Computed Tomography (CT) provides detailed cross-sectional images through advanced imaging techniques. Ultrasound systems use sound waves to capture real-time images, particularly useful in obstetrics and soft tissue examinations. Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI) employs strong magnets and radio waves for high-resolution images of organs. Nuclear imaging involves radioactive tracers to observe physiological processes. Other market segments may include optical and thermographic imaging technologies.</p></p>
