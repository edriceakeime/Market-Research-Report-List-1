<p><ul><li>Acoustic Membranes</li><li>Resonators Panel</li><li>Porous Material Panel</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The building acoustic panel market consists of three main types: acoustic membranes, resonators panels, and porous material panels. Acoustic membranes are thin layers that effectively block sound transmission, making them ideal for walls and ceilings. Resonator panels utilize specific frequencies to absorb sound, enhancing acoustic quality in spaces like theaters. Porous material panels, made from materials like fiberglass or foam, absorb sound waves, reducing echoes and improving overall sound quality in various environments, including offices and concert halls.</p></p>
