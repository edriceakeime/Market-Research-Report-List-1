<p><ul><li>Single-level Ventilator</li><li>Bi-level Ventilator</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Non-Invasive Ventilator for anti-snoring market includes two main types: Single-level ventilators and Bi-level ventilators. Single-level ventilators provide a constant air pressure to keep the airway open, primarily aiding snorers with consistent airflow during sleep. In contrast, Bi-level ventilators adjust pressure levels, offering higher airflow during inhalation and lower during exhalation, enhancing comfort and efficacy for users with varying degrees of airway obstruction. Both types aim to improve sleep quality and alleviate snoring effectively.</p></p>
