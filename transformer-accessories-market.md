Low voltage transformers typically operate below 1 kV, providing power for residential and small commercial applications. High voltage transformers, exceeding 35 kV, are used for transmission and industrial applications, facilitating long-distance power distribution. Medium voltage transformers operate between low and high voltage levels (1 kV to 35 kV), serving commercial and industrial sectors, connecting distribution systems and end users efficiently.</p></p>
