<p><p>The Thoracic Aortic Stent System market comprises primarily two types: Straight Type and Branched Type stents. Straight Type stents are designed for straightforward aortic repairs, providing a linear pathway for blood flow and suitable for uncomplicated cases. Branched Type stents, on the other hand, include additional access points for branches of the aorta, accommodating more complex anatomies and offering versatility for treating conditions involving side branches. Together, these options cater to varying clinical needs in thoracic aortic interventions.</p></p>
