<p><p>The fire bricks market is categorized into two main types: unshaped (monolithic refractories) and shaped refractories. Unshaped refractories, such as castables and ramming mixes, are versatile materials used for custom applications where normal bricks can't fit. Shaped refractories, on the other hand, include pre-formed bricks and blocks tailored for specific industrial uses, offering precise dimensions and consistent performance. Both types are essential for high-temperature applications, ensuring durability and thermal efficiency in various industries like metallurgy, ceramics, and petrochemicals.</p></p>
