<p><p>The synthetic thermoplastic elastomers (TPE) market includes several key types: Styrenic Block Copolymers (SBC) offer flexibility and durability, making them ideal for automotive and consumer goods. Thermoplastic Polyurethane (TPU) is known for its exceptional abrasion resistance and versatility, used in various applications. Thermoplastic Vulcanizates (TPV) blend rubber's elasticity with thermoplastics for improved processing. Thermoplastic Polyvinyl Chloride (TPVC) combines PVC properties with elastomeric characteristics for applications in wiring and tubing. Other categories encompass emerging materials and specialized blends for niche applications.</p></p>
