<p><p>The electric bike insurance market comprises two primary types: property insurance and accident insurance. Property insurance covers damages or losses related to the e-bike itself, including theft, vandalism, and accidental damage. Accident insurance, on the other hand, provides coverage for injuries sustained by the rider or third parties in the event of an accident. Together, these insurance types offer comprehensive protection for e-bike owners, ensuring financial security against potential risks associated with ownership and usage.</p></p>
