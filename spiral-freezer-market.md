<p><p>The spiral freezer market includes two primary types: single drum and double drum spiral freezers. Single drum spiral freezers feature a single continuous drum design, allowing for efficient freezing with a smaller footprint, ideal for operations with limited space. In contrast, double drum spiral freezers employ two interconnected drums, enhancing capacity and throughput, making them suitable for larger production demands. Both systems utilize a helical conveyor to maximize product exposure to cold air, ensuring uniform freezing while maintaining product quality.</p></p>
