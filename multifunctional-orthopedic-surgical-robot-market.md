<p><p>The multifunctional orthopedic surgical robot market is categorized into three main types: semi-active, active, and passive robots. Semi-active robots assist surgeons by providing support and guidance during procedures while allowing for manual input. Active robots autonomously perform specific tasks using advanced algorithms and sensors, enhancing precision and efficiency. Passive robots offer minimal intervention, primarily serving as tools that support traditional surgical methods without automated functions. Each type plays a crucial role in improving surgical outcomes and efficiency in orthopedic procedures.</p></p>
