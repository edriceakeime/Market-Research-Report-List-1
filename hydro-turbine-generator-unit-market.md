<p><strong>The Hydro Turbine Generator Unit Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Impulse Turbines</li><li>Reaction Turbines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hydro turbine generator unit market encompasses two main turbine types: impulse and reaction turbines. Impulse turbines convert kinetic energy from water jets, making them ideal for high-head applications with low flow. In contrast, reaction turbines utilize the pressure from flowing water, suitable for lower-head, high-flow scenarios. Both types play crucial roles in hydropower generation, impacting renewable energy production, efficiency, and sustainability. Their applications span various scales, from small rural projects to large hydroelectric plants, driving growth in the renewable energy sector.</p></p>
